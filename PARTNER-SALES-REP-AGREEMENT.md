## 1. ROLE

Representative will identify, engage, and close home service company partnerships on behalf of UpTend. Representative is an independent contractor, not an employee.

## 2. RESPONSIBILITIES

Representative agrees to:
- Identify and approach qualified home service companies (HVAC primary, with expansion to plumbing, electrical, and other verticals)
- Conduct in-person discovery meetings using UpTend's George AI discovery tool (uptendapp.com/discovery)
- Present UpTend's partner packages and close signed agreements
- Facilitate onboarding introductions between new partners and UpTend
- Provide industry insight to improve George's service-specific knowledge
- Maintain professional representation of the UpTend brand
- Report pipeline activity monthly

## 3. COMPENSATION

### Setup Fee Commission
Representative earns **20%** of the one-time setup fee for each partner they close.

| Partner Tier | Setup Fee | Rep Commission |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | $1,500 | $300 |
| Growth | $2,500 | $500 |
| Dominate | $5,000 | $1,000 |

### Monthly Recurring Commission
Representative earns a percentage of the monthly subscription fee for each partner they close:

- **Months 1 through 12:** 10% of monthly subscription
- **Month 13 onward:** 5% of monthly subscription (ongoing residual)

| Partner Tier | Monthly Fee | Months 1-12 | Month 13+ |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $297/mo | $29.70/mo | $14.85/mo |
| Growth | $597/mo | $59.70/mo | $29.85/mo |
| Dominate | $997/mo | $99.70/mo | $49.85/mo |

### Social Media Add-On Commission
If partner adds social media management ($500/mo):
- **Months 1 through 12:** 10% ($50/mo)
- **Month 13 onward:** 5% ($25/mo)

### Job Fee Revenue Share
Representative earns **2%** of UpTend's job fee revenue from partners they closed.
(Example: Partner on Growth tier, $500 job = $25 UpTend fee x 2% = $0.50 per job)

## 4. PAYMENT TERMS

- Setup fee commission paid within 15 days of partner's first payment
- Monthly recurring commission paid on the 15th of each month for the prior month
- Job fee revenue share paid monthly with recurring commission
- Minimum payout threshold: $50 (balances below $50 roll to next month)

## 5. ATTRIBUTION

- A partner is attributed to Representative if Representative was the primary point of contact for the initial discovery meeting
- Attribution is permanent for that partner relationship
- If a partner cancels and later re-signs, original attribution is maintained for 12 months after cancellation

## 7. TOOLS PROVIDED BY UPTEND

## 8. EXCLUSIVITY

This agreement is non-exclusive. Representative may work with other companies, provided they do not directly compete with UpTend's partner services in the same territory.

UpTend may engage additional sales representatives in the same territory.

## 9. TERM

- Initial term: 12 months from effective date
- Auto-renews for 12-month periods unless either party provides 30 days written notice
- Recurring commissions for existing partners continue for 6 months after termination
- Setup fee commissions for deals in pipeline at termination are honored if closed within 60 days

## 10. CONFIDENTIALITY

Representative agrees to keep confidential all partner pricing, margins, internal tools, and business strategies shared by UpTend. This obligation survives termination.

## 11. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR

Representative is an independent contractor. Representative is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and expenses. UpTend will issue a 1099 for annual earnings exceeding $600.
