## ARTICLE 3: LICENSE FEES

### Section 3.1 — Fee Structure

In consideration of the License granted hereunder, Licensee shall pay to Licensor the following fees, calculated as a percentage of Gross Revenue:

(a) **Technology License Fee:** Fifteen percent (15%) of Gross Revenue from SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts — for access to and use of the Platform, including all software, AI systems, compliance tools, and technology infrastructure;

(b) **Workforce Access Fee:** Ten percent (10%) of Gross Revenue from SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts — for access to the Pro Network, including Pro vetting, certification, training, and availability;

(c) **Operations Fee:** Five percent (5%) of Gross Revenue from SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts — for operational support including dispatch coordination, accounting integration, compliance notifications, and reporting;

(d) **Total License Fee:** Thirty percent (30%) of Gross Revenue from SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts (collectively, the "License Fees").

The foregoing fee structure is summarized in **Exhibit A** (Fee Schedule) attached hereto.

### Section 3.2 — Gross Revenue Definition

For the purposes of calculating License Fees:

(a) "Gross Revenue" means the total amount received by Licensee from government agencies for work and services performed under SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts, before any deductions for expenses, fees, Pro payments, taxes, or other obligations.

(b) Gross Revenue includes, without limitation: fixed-price contract payments, cost-reimbursable contract payments, time-and-materials payments, incentive fees, award fees, bonuses, and any other amounts received in connection with SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts.

(c) Gross Revenue does NOT include: tax refunds unrelated to contract performance, insurance proceeds for property damage, or amounts received in connection with the disposition of Company assets (other than in the ordinary course of business).

### Section 3.3 — Calculation and Payment

(a) License Fees shall be calculated monthly based on Gross Revenue received during the preceding calendar month.

(b) License Fees shall be due and payable within fifteen (15) days following the end of each calendar month.

(c) Each payment shall be accompanied by a written report setting forth the calculation of Gross Revenue and License Fees for the applicable month, in a format reasonably acceptable to Licensor.

### Section 3.4 — Priority of Payment

License Fees are the FIRST obligation to be paid from Licensee's revenue, senior in priority to all other expenses, obligations, compensation, and distributions, with the sole exception of government-mandated costs that are required by applicable law or regulation to be paid first (e.g., required withholding taxes, court-ordered garnishments).

### Section 3.5 — Late Payment

(a) Any License Fee not paid when due shall bear interest at the rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month (eighteen percent (18%) per annum), or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is less, from the due date until paid in full.

(b) Late payment interest shall be compounded monthly.

(c) Licensee shall reimburse Licensor for all costs and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) incurred in collecting overdue License Fees.

### Section 3.6 — Fee Adjustment

(a) The License Fee rates set forth in Section 3.1 shall be fixed and locked for the initial ten (10) year term of this Agreement, subject only to annual CPI adjustments as provided in Section 3.6(b).

(b) Commencing on the first anniversary of the Effective Date and annually thereafter, the License Fee rates shall be adjusted upward (but not downward) by the lesser of (i) the percentage increase in the Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers (CPI-U), U.S. City Average, as published by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics for the most recently completed twelve-month period, or (ii) three percent (3%).

(c) After the expiration of the initial ten (10) year term, the License Fee rates may be renegotiated by mutual written agreement of the Parties.

## ARTICLE 4: PRO NETWORK AND WORKFORCE

### Section 4.1 — Pro Relationships

(a) All independent contractor (Pro) relationships are exclusively with Licensor. Each Pro has executed an Independent Contractor Agreement directly with Licensor.

(b) Licensee does not and shall not independently contract with, engage, hire, or retain any service professionals for work to be performed under SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts, except through the Platform and Pro Network.

(c) Nothing in this Agreement creates or implies any employment or independent contractor relationship between Licensee and any Pro.

### Section 4.2 — Pro Management

(a) Licensor is solely responsible for all Pro onboarding, background checks, certification, training, ICA management, tax reporting (IRS Form 1099), and compliance with applicable labor and employment laws.

(b) Licensor shall maintain the Pro Network in a manner sufficient to support Licensee's SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract operations, including maintaining an adequate number of qualified Pros in the geographic areas where Licensee performs SDVOSB Set-Aside Contracts.

(c) Licensor shall ensure that all Pros assigned to SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract Work Orders meet the qualifications, certifications, and clearance requirements specified in the applicable SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract.

### Section 4.3 — Work Order Process

(a) Licensee may request specific certified Pros for SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract Work Orders through the Platform's dispatch system.

(b) Pro assignment shall be subject to Pro availability, qualifications, and acceptance of the Work Order through the Platform.

(c) Pro payment amounts for SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract Work Orders shall be determined by each Pro's accepted flat-rate quote on the applicable Work Order, as submitted and accepted through the Platform.

### Section 4.4 — Pro Payments

(a) Pro payments for SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract Work Orders shall be processed by Licensor through Stripe Connect or such other payment system as Licensor may designate.

(b) The standard payment schedule for SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract Work Orders shall be: fifty percent (50%) of the quoted amount paid to the Pro upon assignment and acceptance of the Work Order, and fifty percent (50%) paid upon verified completion of the work.

(c) Licensor retains its standard platform fees from Pro payments in accordance with the Pro's ICA.

(d) Licensee acknowledges that Pro payment amounts are determined by market rates and Pro negotiations through the Platform, and that Licensor has no obligation to ensure specific pricing or availability.

## ARTICLE 5: PAYMENT FLOW

### Section 5.1 — SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract Payment Flow

The Parties agree to the following payment flow for SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract revenues:

**Step 1:** Government agency remits payment to Licensee for services rendered under the applicable SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract.

**Step 2:** Licensee remits License Fees (30% of Gross Revenue) to Licensor within fifteen (15) days of receipt of government payment.

**Step 3:** Licensee remits Pro payment amounts to Licensor (or Licensor processes Pro payments directly through the Platform's Stripe Connect infrastructure) in accordance with the Work Order payment schedule.

**Step 4:** Licensor pays Pros via Stripe Connect (50% upon assignment, 50% upon verified completion).

**Step 5:** Licensee retains the remainder for operating expenses, reserves, and other obligations as permitted under the LLC Operating Agreement.

### Section 5.2 — Monthly Reconciliation

(a) The Parties shall conduct a detailed monthly reconciliation of all SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract revenues, License Fees, Pro payments, and other amounts within twenty (20) days following the end of each calendar month.

(b) The reconciliation shall include:

(i) Total Gross Revenue received from each SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract;

(ii) License Fees due and paid;

(iii) Pro payment amounts processed;

(iv) Any adjustments, credits, or disputed amounts; and

(v) Net amounts retained by Licensee.

(c) Any discrepancies identified in the reconciliation shall be resolved and corrected within ten (10) business days.

### Section 5.3 — Separate Accounts

Licensee shall maintain separate bank accounts for SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract receipts and shall not commingle SDVOSB Set-Aside Contract funds with any other funds.

(a) The License granted hereunder shall immediately cease (or, in the case of termination with a notice period, at the end of the applicable notice period);

(b) Licensee shall immediately cease all use of the Platform, Pro Network, and all Licensor intellectual property;

The following provisions shall survive termination of this Agreement: Article 1 (Definitions), Article 9 (Representations and Warranties, to the extent they relate to post-termination obligations), Article 10 (Liability and Indemnification), Article 11 (Confidentiality), Article 12 (Governing Law and Disputes), and any other provision that by its nature is intended to survive termination.
