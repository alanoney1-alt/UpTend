The Home DNA Scan is our Trojan horse. It's free, it's novel, and it creates demand.

**Flow:**
1. Customer uploads photos of their home (exterior + interior)
2. George analyzes and generates a "Home Health Report" â€” identifies maintenance needs, estimates costs, prioritizes by urgency

**Viral mechanics:**
- **Shareability:** "Home Health Score" is shareable (like a credit score for your house). *"My home scored 72/100 â€” what's yours?"*
- **Neighborhood comparison:** *"Homes in Laureate Park average 78/100. Yours is 65. Here's why."*
- **Social proof prompt:** After scan, George says: *"Want to share your Home Health Score? Send this link to neighbors â€” they get a free scan and $25, you get $25."*
- **Seasonal triggers:** George emails/texts: *"Hurricane season is coming. Your home scan showed your gutters need attention. Want to get ahead of it?"*
