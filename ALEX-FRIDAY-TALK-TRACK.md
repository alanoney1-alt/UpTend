# Alex Friday Lunch — Talk Track
### Comfort Solutions Tech LLC — HVAC Partnership Pitch

---

## The Setup (2 minutes)

"Alex, I'm not here to sell you anything. I'm here to solve problems. You told me Monday you're running around doing 3 jobs, you need another employee, you don't have time for marketing, and you're wasting gas driving to give quotes. I built something that fixes all of that. Let me show you."

---

## The AI Photo Quote (3 minutes — DEMO THIS)

"Remember you said if customers could just send you a picture of their unit, you could quote over the phone? Here's the link. Text this to your next customer."

*Show him the link on your phone: uptendapp.com/partners/comfort-solutions-tech/quote*

"Customer clicks it. Name, email, phone, address — 10 seconds. Takes 1-3 photos of their unit. Our AI identifies the make, model, year, condition, any visible issues. All of that goes straight to your dashboard. You call them, give the quote over the phone. No truck roll. No wasted gas. No wasted afternoon."

**Let him react. Don't keep talking.**

---

## The Marketing (3 minutes)

"You told me your marketing budget is about 12% of revenue. What are you spending it on right now?"

*Listen. Then:*

"Here's what I can do. I'm going to build your online presence from zero to competitive in 30 days:
- Your own landing page on our platform
- SEO pages targeting the neighborhoods where you want to work — Lake Nona, Windermere, Winter Park, wherever
- Daily blog posts building your search rankings
- Social media content created and posted for you
- All of it reviewed by our CMO Chelsea — real human, real marketing brain, not just AI"

---

## The 6-Part Competitive Audit (THIS IS THE CLOSER)

"But here's what we do that nobody else does. On your first day as a partner, we run a complete competitive audit of your local market. Six deep-dive analyses:"

1. **Review Teardown** — "We scrape your top 3 competitors' Google reviews. How many they're getting per month, what services customers mention, what they're complaining about. You'll know exactly how many reviews you need per month to catch the leader and how long it'll take."

2. **Service Area Page Gap** — "We crawl every competitor's website and find every service+neighborhood page they have that you don't. Then we build those pages for you."

3. **Google Business Profile Category Audit** — "Most guys set up their GBP once and never touch it. We find every category your competitors have that you're missing. I've seen businesses add one category and start showing up for a whole new set of searches the next week."

4. **Photo Audit** — "Google says businesses with photos get 42% more direction requests and 35% more clicks to their website. We count your photos vs. competitors and give you an 8-week photo plan. George even texts you weekly reminders — 'This week: upload 3 before-and-after shots.'"

5. **Citation Cleanup** — "If your business name is listed differently across Yelp, BBB, Google, and Facebook, Google trusts you less. We check every directory and give you a prioritized cleanup list."

6. **Backlink Analysis** — "We find every website that links to your competitors but not you. Those are warm targets. They already link to HVAC businesses. We reach out and get you those links."

**"Most agencies charge $2,000-$5,000 just for this audit. It's included on day one."**

*Let that land.*

---

## The Offer (2 minutes)

"Here's what I want to do. I'm going to run all of this for you for free. Three months. Full case study. You don't pay a dime."

"What I get out of it: I get to prove this works. I track everything — every impression, every lead, every dollar. At the end of 3 months, we look at the numbers together."

"If we beat what you're doing now — which we will — then we talk about making it permanent. $499 a month. That's it. Plus whatever leads we generate for you, we take a small marketing fee on the ones we created. You'll still be spending less than you are now and getting 10x more."

---

## The Close (30 seconds)

Don't close. Let him close himself.

"So... you want to try it?"

---

## If He Asks About Price Details

- Setup: $1,000 (waived for case study)
- Monthly: $499/mo (waived for 3 months)  
- Marketing fee: 5% on leads WE generate (trackable through our links)
- His own customers on the platform: $30 credit to him when they book their first job through us

## If He Asks "What's the Catch?"

"The catch is I need you to actually use it. Give me feedback. Tell me what's working and what's not. And let me use your results to sell the next 10 HVAC companies."

## If He Asks About George

"George is our AI. He answers your calls, chats, and texts 24/7. Nights, weekends, holidays. He qualifies the lead, captures the details, and puts it on your dashboard. You never miss another call."

## DO NOT

- Feature dump. Show the photo quote, mention the audit, make the offer. That's it.
- Talk about the full UpTend platform, HOA stuff, government stuff, or anything beyond his business
- Get technical about AI, models, or how it works under the hood
- Mention pricing tiers, à la carte menus, or bundle discounts — that comes later
- Talk more than him. This is a conversation, not a presentation.
