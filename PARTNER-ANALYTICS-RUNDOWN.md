# UpTend Partner Analytics
### How We Track Everything — From Impression to Revenue

---

## The Full Funnel

Every dollar a partner makes through UpTend is traceable back to the moment someone first saw their name. Here's how we track each step.

---

## 1. SEO — Search Engine Visibility

**What we track:**
- How many times their pages appeared in Google search results (impressions)
- How many people clicked through to their page (clicks)
- Which keywords are driving traffic ("HVAC repair Lake Nona," "AC service Windermere")
- Search position for each keyword over time
- Which neighborhood pages are performing best

**How we track it:**
- Server-side analytics on every page visit (no cookie consent needed)
- Google Search Console API for search impression and click data
- Keyword ranking reports updated weekly

**What the partner sees on their dashboard:**
- "Your pages appeared in 4,200 Google searches this month"
- "312 people clicked through to your pages"
- "You rank #3 for 'AC repair Lake Nona'"
- Trend charts showing growth over time

---

## 2. Social Media — Content Performance

**What we track:**
- Impressions (how many people saw each post)
- Reach (unique accounts that saw the content)
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares, saves)
- Link clicks from posts back to the partner's UpTend page
- Video views and watch time
- Follower growth
- Best performing content types and posting times

**How we track it:**
- Facebook Page Insights API (impressions, reach, engagement per post)
- Instagram Insights API (reach, profile visits, website clicks, saves)
- TikTok Analytics API (video views, engagement, follower growth)
- UTM parameters on every link so we know exactly which post drove which visit

**What the partner sees on their dashboard:**
- "Your 20 posts this month reached 15,000 people"
- "400 clicked through to your page"
- "Best performing post: 'Spring AC tune-up checklist' — 2,300 impressions, 47 engagements"
- Monthly content performance report

---

## 3. George AI — Conversations and Lead Capture

**What we track:**
- Total chat sessions started on partner pages
- Conversations completed vs. abandoned
- Lead information captured (name, email, phone, address, service needed)
- Photo quote submissions (photos uploaded, AI analysis completed)
- Appointments booked through George
- After-hours conversations (leads that would have been missed without George)
- Average response time (instant — George never sleeps)

**How we track it:**
- Every George conversation is logged with full transcript
- Lead data stored in partner's dashboard with source attribution
- Photo quotes tracked from upload through AI analysis to partner notification
- Timestamps on every interaction

**What the partner sees on their dashboard:**
- "George handled 47 conversations this month"
- "32 leads captured with full contact info"
- "8 photo quotes submitted"
- "14 conversations happened after business hours — leads you would have lost"

---

## 4. Phone Calls — Voice Lead Tracking

**What we track:**
- Total inbound calls to the partner's dedicated number
- Call duration
- Call recordings (for quality and training)
- Calls answered by George AI vs. forwarded to the partner
- Source attribution (which marketing channel drove the call)
- After-hours calls handled
- Calls converted to booked jobs

**How we track it:**
- Twilio call tracking with unique phone numbers per channel
- George AI voice handles calls with full conversation logging
- Call source identified by which number was dialed (SEO number vs. social number vs. direct)

**What the partner sees on their dashboard:**
- "34 calls this month on your dedicated line"
- "12 were after hours — George handled them all"
- "22 calls converted to booked appointments"
- "Average call duration: 4 minutes"
- Breakdown by source: 18 from SEO, 9 from social, 7 direct

---

## 5. Lead Generation — Outbound Prospecting

**What we track:**
- Leads scraped from online sources (Facebook groups, Reddit, Craigslist, Nextdoor)
- People posting about needing HVAC service in the partner's area
- Bot replies posted pointing to the partner's page
- Click-throughs from those replies (UTM tracked)
- Leads that converted from scraped sources

**How we track it:**
- n8n automation workflows scraping and logging every lead found
- UTM parameters on all outbound links
- Conversion tracking from first touch to booked job

**What the partner sees on their dashboard:**
- "We found 23 people online looking for HVAC service in your area this month"
- "15 were contacted or directed to your page"
- "4 became leads, 2 booked jobs"

---

## 6. Reviews — Reputation Building

**What we track:**
- Review requests sent (automated after every completed job)
- Reviews received (Google, Yelp, Facebook)
- Average star rating and trend
- Review response rate
- Negative review alerts

**How we track it:**
- Automated SMS/email review requests via George after job completion
- Google Business Profile API for review monitoring
- Alerts pushed to partner dashboard and phone

**What the partner sees on their dashboard:**
- "12 review requests sent this month"
- "8 new Google reviews received (4.9 average)"
- "Your rating improved from 4.7 to 4.9 this quarter"

---

## 7. Jobs and Revenue — The Bottom Line

**What we track:**
- Jobs booked through the platform
- Job status (scheduled, in progress, completed)
- Revenue per job
- Total monthly revenue generated through UpTend
- Revenue by source (which channel generated each job)
- Average ticket size
- Close rate (leads to booked jobs)

**How we track it:**
- Full job lifecycle tracked in the platform
- Revenue attributed to original lead source
- Invoicing and payment processing through the dashboard

**What the partner sees on their dashboard:**
- "18 jobs completed this month"
- "Total revenue: $24,600"
- "Average ticket: $1,367"
- "Close rate: 56% (leads to jobs)"
- Revenue breakdown: 8 from SEO, 5 from George chat, 3 from phone, 2 from social
