## Money-Saving Strategies for Orlando Residents

### Timing Your Cleanout
**Best Pricing Months**: January-March, October-November
**Avoid Premium Periods**: May-August (college move-outs), September (hurricane prep)
**Weekday Discounts**: Many companies offer 10-20% off Monday-Thursday

### DIY Preparation Tips
1. **Sort items beforehand** - reduces labor time
2. **Move items to accessible location** - avoid access fees
3. **Separate recyclables** - some companies reduce fees for sorted materials
4. **Group similar items** - increases efficiency
5. **Take photos** - helps with accurate estimates

### Bundle Services for Savings
- **Annual contracts**: 15-25% discount for regular service
- **Multiple properties**: Volume discounts for landlords
- **Referral programs**: $25-75 credit for successful referrals
- **Seasonal packages**: Discounted rates for planned cleanouts

### Compare Like-for-Like Quotes
When getting estimates, ensure quotes include:
- **All labor costs** (loading, sorting, hauling)
- **Disposal fees** at appropriate facilities
- **Environmental charges** for special items
- **Travel time** to your location
- **Any minimum charges** or trip fees

## What Affects Your Final Price

### Item Types & Disposal Requirements
**Easy Disposal (Lower Cost)**:
- Wood furniture, cardboard, metal scraps
- Books, clothes, general household items
- Yard debris, construction materials

**Complex Disposal (Higher Cost)**:
- Electronics, appliances with Freon
- Mattresses, tires, automotive fluids
- Paint, chemicals, fluorescent bulbs

### Volume Estimation Accuracy
**Overestimation Costs**: Paying for unused truck space
**Underestimation Costs**: Additional trips or upgraded service level

**Pro Tip**: Most reputable companies offer on-site estimates to avoid surprises.

### Labor Intensity Factors
- **Heavy items**: Furniture, appliances, exercise equipment
- **Delicate items**: Requiring careful handling and protection
- **Sorting required**: Mixed debris needing separation
- **Difficult access**: Stairs, narrow hallways, tight corners
