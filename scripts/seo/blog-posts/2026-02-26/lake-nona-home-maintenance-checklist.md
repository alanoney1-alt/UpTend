## Monthly Home Maintenance Tasks for Lake Nona Residents

### HVAC System Care (Critical in Florida Heat)
- **Replace air filters** every 30-60 days (more frequently during construction seasons)
- **Check ductwork** for leaks that increase energy costs
- **Inspect outdoor units** for debris, vegetation overgrowth
- **Schedule professional maintenance** twice yearly (before summer/winter)

Average cost savings: $200-500 annually in energy bills

### Gutter and Drainage Management
Lake Nona's newer homes often feature seamless gutters, but they still require attention:
- **Clean gutters** monthly during rainy season (June-October)
- **Check downspouts** for proper drainage away from foundation
- **Inspect gutter guards** if installed
- **Verify proper grading** around home perimeter

*Professional gutter cleaning in Lake Nona typically runs $150-225 for 1-2 story homes*

### Exterior Maintenance
- **Pressure wash driveways and walkways** monthly (high foot traffic areas)
- **Inspect roof shingles** for loose, damaged, or missing pieces
- **Check exterior caulking** around windows, doors, and trim
- **Clean and inspect pool area** if applicable
- **Trim vegetation** away from home exterior (minimum 3 feet clearance)

## Seasonal Maintenance Schedule for Central Florida

### Spring (March-May)
- Deep clean and inspect air conditioning system
- Power wash exterior surfaces
- Check irrigation systems and sprinkler heads
- Inspect and repair any winter storm damage
- Service pool equipment

### Summer (June-August) - Hurricane Preparedness
- Trim trees and remove dead branches
- Check hurricane shutters and emergency supplies
- Inspect roof and gutters thoroughly
- Test generator if installed
- Review insurance coverage

### Fall (September-November) - Post-Hurricane Season
- Repair any storm damage immediately
- Clean and inspect HVAC system
- Check weatherstripping and insulation
- Prepare landscape for cooler months

### Winter (December-February)
- Service heating system (yes, even in Florida!)
- Inspect and clean fireplace if present
- Check for pest intrusion points
- Plan major renovation projects for spring
