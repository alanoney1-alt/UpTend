### 3. Electrical Hazards
Never attempt DIY electrical repairs. These require immediate professional attention:

- Power outages (partial home)

- Tripped breakers that won't reset
- Burning smells from electrical panels

**Safety First:** If you smell burning, evacuate and call 911

- Roof leaks from wind damage
- Window/door damage from debris
- Fence repairs from fallen branches
- Water intrusion from damaged siding

**Hurricane Season:** June 1 - November 30

## Finding Reliable Emergency Service in Orlando

### What to Look for in Emergency Contractors

**Licensing & Insurance**
- Valid Florida contractor license
- Current liability insurance
- Workers' compensation coverage
- Better Business Bureau rating

**Response Commitments**
- Same-day availability
- 24/7 emergency hotline
- Upfront pricing (no "estimate surprises")
- Service area coverage map

**Red Flags to Avoid**
- Door-to-door solicitations after storms
- Demands for full payment upfront
- No local address or references
- Prices significantly below market rate

## Cost-Saving Emergency Tips

### Before You Call
1. **Document everything** - Photos for insurance
2. **Stop the source** - Shut off water/power if safe
3. **Minimize damage** - Move furniture, use tarps
4. **Call your insurance** - Report major damage immediately

### During Service
- **Get written estimates** before work begins
- **Ask about warranties** on emergency repairs
- **Understand temporary vs permanent** solutions
- **Schedule follow-up inspections** for major repairs

## Prevention is the Best Strategy

While emergency services are crucial, the best approach is preventing emergencies altogether:

- **Seasonal HVAC maintenance** (spring and fall)
- **Annual roof inspections** before hurricane season  
- **Quarterly plumbing checks** (especially in older homes)
- **Electrical panel reviews** every 2-3 years

## When to Call Professionals vs DIY

### Always Call Professionals
- Electrical work beyond changing bulbs
- Gas line issues
- Major plumbing (beyond simple clogs)
- Structural damage assessment
- Roof repairs above single-story height

### Safe DIY Temporary Fixes
- Shutting off water valves
- Switching circuit breakers
- Applying weatherproofing tape
- Moving items away from leaks
- Cleaning minor drain clogs
