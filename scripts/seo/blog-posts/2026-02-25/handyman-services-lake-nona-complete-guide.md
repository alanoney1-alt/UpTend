## Seasonal Maintenance Tips for Lake Nona Homes

### Spring (March-May)
- Check and clean gutters after winter storms
- Inspect and repair caulking around windows and doors
- Service HVAC systems before summer heat
- Power wash exterior surfaces

### Summer (June-August)
- Monitor ceiling fans and air circulation
- Check for pest entry points and seal gaps
- Maintain outdoor spaces and equipment
- Keep up with pool maintenance (if applicable)

### Fall (September-November)
- Prepare for hurricane season with loose fixture checks
- Clean and inspect roof areas
- Service heating systems before occasional cold snaps
- Trim trees and bushes away from house

### Winter (December-February)
- Check for drafts and improve insulation where needed
- Maintain fireplaces and heating systems
- Address any storm damage promptly
- Plan spring improvement projects

## DIY vs. Professional: Making the Right Choice

### When to DIY
- Simple tasks like changing light bulbs or air filters
- Basic cleaning and organizing
- Minor touch-up painting on small areas
- Unclogging minor drain issues

### When to Call a Professional
- Electrical work beyond changing fixtures
- Plumbing repairs involving water lines
- Structural modifications or repairs
- Any work requiring permits
- Tasks involving heights or safety risks
- Projects requiring specialized tools
