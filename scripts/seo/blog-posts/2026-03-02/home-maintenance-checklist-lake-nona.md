## Why Lake Nona Homes Need Special Attention

Lake Nona's newer construction and Florida's subtropical climate create unique maintenance considerations. Unlike older Orlando neighborhoods, your home likely features modern systems and materials that require specific care schedules. The humid summers, afternoon thunderstorms, and occasional cold snaps all impact how we approach home maintenance.

## Monthly Home Health Checks

**HVAC System Care**
Check and replace air filters monthly. Lake Nona's construction boom means more dust and debris in the air. Clean filters keep your system running efficiently and your indoor air quality optimal.

**Plumbing Inspection**
Walk through your home checking for leaks under sinks, around toilets, and near water heaters. Florida's hard water and mineral content can cause issues that catch homeowners off guard.

**Exterior Walk-Around**
Inspect your roof, gutters, and siding for storm damage. Lake Nona sits in prime thunderstorm territory, and small issues become big problems fast in Florida weather.

## Quarterly Deep Maintenance

**Gutter Cleaning and Inspection**
Orlando's frequent rains and abundant tree coverage mean gutters fill quickly. Clean gutters prevent water damage to your foundation and landscaping.

**HVAC Professional Service**
Have your system professionally serviced quarterly. This isn't optional in Florida—it's preventive medicine for your home's most expensive system.

**Pressure Washing**
Florida's humidity breeds mold, mildew, and algae. Pressure wash your driveway, walkways, and home exterior to maintain curb appeal and prevent permanent staining.

## Semi-Annual Priority Tasks

**Roof Professional Inspection**
Twice yearly professional roof inspections catch small problems before Florida weather turns them into major repairs.

**Window and Door Seal Check**
Inspect and replace weatherstripping. Properly sealed homes stay comfortable and keep energy costs manageable.

**Landscape Drainage Review**
Ensure proper drainage around your home's foundation. Lake Nona's newer developments generally have good drainage, but settling and landscaping changes can create issues.

## Annual Major Maintenance

**Electrical System Inspection**
Have a licensed electrician inspect your electrical panel, outlets, and major appliances annually.

**Water Heater Service**
Florida's mineral-heavy water is tough on water heaters. Annual professional service extends their life significantly.

**Pest Control Evaluation**
Comprehensive pest control isn't just about comfort—termites and other pests can cause serious structural damage in Florida's climate.
