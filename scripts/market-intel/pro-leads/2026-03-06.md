## Market Signals

1. **Craigslist is PACKED** — Orlando household services had 35+ active listings from the last 24 hours alone. The market is competitive and pros are actively hustling for clients.

2. **Bilingual is the norm** — At least 40% of listings mention "Se habla español." UpTend's bilingual support (or lack thereof) will be a factor in recruitment and customer reach.

3. **No deposit / free estimate is standard** — Almost every independent pro offers free estimates and no deposit. UpTend's guaranteed payment model is a genuine differentiator here — these folks are clearly dealing with no-shows and payment uncertainty.

4. **Pressure washing is underrepresented on CL** — Despite being huge in Florida, most pressure washing companies have their own websites and don't post on Craigslist. They're on Google Maps and Facebook instead. Need browser-based scouting for those.

5. **Cleaning is saturated** — Tons of cleaning services, many solo operators. Good volume opportunity for UpTend but margins may be thin.

6. **East Orlando / Lake Nona gap** — Very few listings specifically mention Lake Nona, St. Cloud, or Narcoossee. This is UpTend's geographic opportunity — serve the underserved area.

7. **Pricing intel:** Painters quoting $0.50/sqft walls or $150/room. Carpet install $1.00-1.75/sqft. Yard cleanup $125-400. These are rock-bottom Craigslist rates.
