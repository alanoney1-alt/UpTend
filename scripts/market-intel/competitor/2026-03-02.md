# Competitor Intelligence Report — March 2, 2026

## 1. Pricing Check (Orlando Market)

### TaskRabbit Orlando
- **Cleaning:** $40+/hr minimum
- **Handyman:** $55/hr average (range $35-$80/hr per Thumbtack data)
- **Moving:** Hourly rates, typically $50-80/hr per mover
- TaskRabbit charges a 15% service fee on top of Tasker rates

### Thumbtack Orlando
- **Handyman:** $35-$80/hr, average ~$55/hr. Minimum fee $50-$100 for small jobs
- **Pressure Washing:** Active local pros (All Around Pressure & Soft Washing FL, Swift 360, IR Handyman). Typical driveway wash $150-$300, full house $200-$500+
- **Top categories:** Handyman, pressure washing, painting, cleaning, junk removal

### Angi/HomeAdvisor Orlando
- Pressure washing house cost guide exists but behind Cloudflare wall. Industry standard Orlando: $150-$400 for standard house wash
- Junk removal: Multiple listed companies (Magic Touch Pressure Washing & Junk Removal, etc.)
- Angi charges pros per lead ($15-$80+ per lead depending on category)

### Google Ads CPC
- Pressure washing keywords in Orlando: **$5-$15 per click**
- Highly competitive market for all home services categories

### UpTend Comparison
- UpTend's 20% total fee (5% customer + 15% pro) is **competitive with TaskRabbit's 15% service fee** plus their markup. UpTend's split is more transparent and pro-friendly since both sides share the cost
- Thumbtack/Angi charge pros per lead regardless of conversion. UpTend only charges on completed jobs — major advantage for pros

## 2. Review Mining — Competitor Pain Points

### Top Complaints (from Reddit, Yelp, BBB)

1. **No vetting / fake credentials** — Angi and Thumbtack do NOT verify contractor licenses. Multiple reports of unqualified people, even felons, being recommended. One homeowner got TWO repeat felons from Thumbtack and Angi
2. **Overpriced quotes** — Angi pros often quote $600-$800 MORE than independent contractors for the same job. Pros pass lead costs to customers
3. **Guarantees are worthless** — "Their guarantees are a joke. They do everything in their power to keep from reimbursing you"
4. **Lead selling, not quality matching** — "Their business model isn't giving you quality, it's charging companies to advertise to you and selling your data"
5. **Pro frustration** — Pros hate paying for leads that don't convert. "Angi Ads ROI is so terrible, I strongly recommend to other handymen and pros to stay away"
6. **Best Home Services (Orlando local)** — Yelp reviews say they "charge 4-5x what others will while smiling at your ignorance and give below average repairs"
