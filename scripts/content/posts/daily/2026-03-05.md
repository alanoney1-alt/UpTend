## 3. Facebook Group Comment Template (Lake Nona / Orlando Groups)

**For posts asking: "Anyone know a good electrician?" or "Outlet sparking, what do I do?"**

Just wanted to share a few things that might help while you are looking.

If an outlet is sparking or warm to the touch, stop using it right away and do not plug anything else in. That is usually a sign of a loose or failing connection inside the wall.

If your breaker keeps tripping on the same circuit, it is doing its job but it is telling you something needs attention. Resetting it over and over can actually make the problem worse.

When you do call someone, ask them to check your panel brand and the age of your wiring. In a lot of Orlando homes built in the 80s and 90s, the panels themselves are the weak link.

Hope that helps. This stuff is too important to just wing it.
