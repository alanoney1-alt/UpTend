"Neural Networks" vs. "Quotes": We replaced the boring "Get a Quote" with "AI Vision Quoting." It sounds faster, fairer, and more precise.

"Operating System" vs. "Service": This elevates you above the guy with a truck. An OS is essential; a service is optional.

"Co-Pilot" vs. "Gig": For workers, we frame the algorithm as their partner, not their boss. It helps them earn, rather than just assigning them tasks.

Visuals: The dark mode sections and "code snippet" UI elements signal to the user: This is a tech company, not a junk company.
