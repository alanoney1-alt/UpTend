Here is the updated Modular Worker ID Card.

I have modified the logic so that the "Digital Handshake" (Video/Bio) is optional.

If the Pycker skips it: The customer sees a clean, professional ID card with just the Name, Rating, and Safety Code.

If the Pycker fills it out: They get a "Trusted Personality" Badge displayed next to their name, and the "View Full Profile" button appears.

üß† The Logic Explained
The "If" Statement: const hasDigitalHandshake = worker?.bio && worker?.funFact;

This one line controls the whole UI. If those fields are missing in the database, the fancy stuff hides automatically.

The Incentive:

Without it: The worker looks like a "Generic Employee" (Safe, but boring).

With it: The worker gets the "Trusted Pro" Blue Badge right next to their name.

Psychology: Workers will want to fill this out because they know customers pick the person with the Badge/Video.
