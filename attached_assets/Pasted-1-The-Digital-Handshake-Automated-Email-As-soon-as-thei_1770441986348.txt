5. The "First Job" Protocol (Quality Control)
You are nervous about the first job. You should be.

The Feature: "Rookie Mode"

Logic: For a worker's first 3 jobs, the app automatically flags them for "Manual Review."

Process:

Worker uploads "After" photo.

Payout is paused for 1 hour.

Admin (You) gets a notification: "Rookie completed Job #1. Check photos."

You look at the photo. If it's good, you click "Approve Payout."

Worker gets a notification: "Great job! Payment released."

Why this works: It catches bad apples immediately before they ruin your reputation. After 3 good jobs, the system switches them to "Auto-Pay."
