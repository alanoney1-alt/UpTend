2. The "Verification Engine" (Pro App Input)
To make the dashboard above true, the Pro needs an easy way to log the data. When a Pro completes a job, they see this ESG Log screen:

Select Action: [Recycled / Donated / Repurposed]

Select Material: [Metal / Wood / Cardboard / E-Waste]

Enter Weight: (e.g., "140 lbs")

Upload Proof: (A photo of the recycling facility receipt or a 360¬∞ scan of the empty space).

Behind the Scenes: As soon as the Pro hits "Submit," your backend runs the CO2 calculation and updates the esg_ledger table. This triggers a notification to the homeowner: "Impact Verified: You just avoided 42kg of CO2 emissions!"
