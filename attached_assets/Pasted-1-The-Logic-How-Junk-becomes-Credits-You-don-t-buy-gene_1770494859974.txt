3. The Revenue Split (The "UpTend Take")
This is how the money moves:

Customer Pays: $14.99 for the "Carbon Neutral Upgrade."

Wholesale Credit Cost: ~$4.00 (Purchased from verified local carbon sinks or offsets).

Pro Incentive: $2.00 (Paid to the Pro for ensuring 100% recycling compliance and uploading the weight slip).

UpTend Profit: $8.99 (Pure Margin).
