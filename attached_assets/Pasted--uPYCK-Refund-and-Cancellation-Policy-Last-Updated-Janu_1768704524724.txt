# uPYCK Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Last Updated:** January 17, 2026

## Overview

This Refund and Cancellation Policy explains how refunds and cancellations work on the uPYCK platform. This policy is part of our Terms of Service.

## Customer Cancellations

### Before PYCKER Accepts

**Free Cancellation**

- You may cancel for free anytime before a PYCKER accepts your job
- Payment authorization will be released immediately
- No charges to your card

### After PYCKER Accepts (But Before Arrival)

**$25 Cancellation Fee**

- Once a PYCKER accepts and is en route, a $25 cancellation fee applies
- This compensates the PYCKER for time and fuel
- Remaining authorized amount released to you

### PYCKER Has Arrived

**$50 Cancellation Fee**

- If PYCKER arrives at your location and you cancel, a $50 fee applies
- This covers the PYCKER’s time, fuel, and lost opportunity
- Remaining authorized amount released to you

### After Service Begins

**Full Charge**

- Once service has begun (loading has started), full job charge applies
- No refund for partially completed work
- You are responsible for full payment

## PYCKER Cancellations

### PYCKER Cancels Before Arrival

**Full Refund**

- If PYCKER cancels for any reason, you receive a full refund
- Payment authorization released immediately
- PYCKER charged $25 penalty (credited to you if service rebooked)

### PYCKER No-Show

**Full Refund + Priority Rebooking**

- If PYCKER doesn’t arrive within 30 minutes of scheduled window
- Full refund issued
- PYCKER charged $25 penalty
- You receive priority matching for next booking

## No-Show Situations

### Customer No-Show

**Full Charge**

- If you are not available at scheduled time
- PYCKER waits 15 minutes and attempts contact
- Full job charge applies if you don’t respond
- No refund issued

### PYCKER Late Arrival

**Partial Refund or Discount**

- PYCKER more than 30 minutes late: 10% discount
- PYCKER more than 60 minutes late: 25% discount
- PYCKER no-show (2+ hours late): Full refund

## Service Quality Issues

### Incomplete or Poor Service

If you are unsatisfied with service quality:

**Report Within 24 Hours:**

- Email support@upyck.com
- Provide photos or video evidence
- Describe the issue in detail

**Resolution Options:**

1. **Partial Refund:** For partially completed work
1. **Full Refund:** For work not done as promised
1. **Rescheduled Service:** Free return visit to complete job
1. **Credit:** Platform credit for future bookings

**Not Eligible for Refund:**

- Change of mind after service completion
- Items you wanted kept were disposed of (without PYCKER being informed)
- Normal wear and tear on property during service
- Difficulty accessing items (unless misrepresented by you)

### Property Damage

**PYCKER Caused Damage:**

- Report immediately (within 24 hours)
- Provide photo evidence
- For Verified Pro PYCKERs: File claim with their insurance
- For Independent PYCKERs: Work with PYCKER directly (uPYCK facilitates but is not responsible)
- uPYCK mediates disputes but does not guarantee resolution

**Pre-existing Damage:**

- Not covered
- Report any pre-existing damage to PYCKER before service begins

## Price Adjustments

### On-Site Price Changes

**Acceptable Changes:**

- Additional items found on-site (you agree to add them)
- Heavier load than estimated
- Stairs not disclosed in booking
- Access difficulties not mentioned

**Unacceptable Changes:**

- Arbitrary price increases
- Pressure to pay more than quoted
- Hidden fees not disclosed upfront

**Your Rights:**

- Accept the new price and proceed
- Decline additional services
- Cancel (subject to cancellation fees)
- Dispute the charge (we’ll investigate)

### Quote Accuracy

**AI Estimates:**

- Photo-based estimates are close but not guaranteed exact
- Final price based on actual volume
- If significantly different (20%+), we’ll investigate
- Refund difference if estimate was unreasonably inaccurate

## Refund Processing

### Timeline

**Payment Authorization Release:** Immediate (within minutes)
**Refund to Card:** 5-10 business days (depends on your bank)
**Platform Credits:** Immediate (applied to your account instantly)

### Refund Method

Refunds issued to original payment method:

- Credit/debit card: Refunded to card
- Platform credit: Applied to account balance
- Cash payments: Not accepted (N/A)

## Tips

**Tips Are Non-Refundable**

- Tips are paid directly to PYCKERs (100%, no platform fee)
- Tips cannot be refunded once paid
- If service quality was poor, request refund of base fare, not tip

**Exception:**
If PYCKER no-shows or cancels, any tip paid is refunded.

## Disputes

### Dispute Process

1. **Contact Support:** Email support@upyck.com within 24 hours
1. **Provide Details:** Photos, description, booking ID
1. **Investigation:** We review within 1-3 business days
1. **Resolution:** Refund, credit, rescheduled service, or denied

### Chargeback Policy

**Before Filing Chargeback:**
Contact us first at support@upyck.com. Most issues are resolved quickly.

**If You File Chargeback:**

- Your account may be suspended pending investigation
- Abuse of chargebacks may result in permanent ban
- Legitimate chargebacks will be honored

## Green Verified Rebates

**PYCKER Rebates Only:**

- Green Verified rebates are for PYCKERs, not customers
- Customers benefit indirectly through verified eco-friendly disposal
- Rebates paid to PYCKERs within 7 days of approval

## Special Circumstances

### Weather or Emergencies

**Severe Weather:**

- Free rescheduling if unsafe to travel
- Applies to both customers and PYCKERs
- No cancellation fees

**Emergencies:**

- Medical emergencies, family emergencies: free cancellation
- Contact us immediately
- We may request documentation

### Recurring Jobs

**Business Account Cancellations:**

- Cancel recurring job with 48 hours notice: no fee
- Less than 48 hours notice: one job charge
- Cancel account: no penalty after current billing cycle

## Contact for Refunds

**Email:** support@upyck.com
**Subject Line:** “Refund Request - [Booking ID]”

**Include:**

- Your name and account email
- Booking ID
- Reason for refund request
- Any supporting documentation (photos, screenshots)

**Response Time:** Within 24-48 hours (business days)
