üß† The Logic of the Checklist
High-Contrast Status: Using AlertCircle in red for the "Home Audit" (the missing insurance shield) creates immediate urgency.

Professional Jargon: We don't say "clean gutters"; we say "Downspout Flow Test." This makes the service feel technical and "Certified."

Proximity Social Proof: The footer mentions "Pros are currently nearby." In Florida, everyone knows that when a storm is coming, contractors disappear. Mentioning availability now is a massive relief.

Value Re-statement: The final button reminds them exactly what they are buying: the $8,400+ Insurance Shield.
