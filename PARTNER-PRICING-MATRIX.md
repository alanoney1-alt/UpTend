# UpTend Partner Pricing — March 2026
## Consultative Pricing: Base + Build Your Stack

No tiers. No boxes. Every partnership starts with the base, then Chelsea and Matt build a custom package based on what the business actually needs and what their budget allows. The more they bundle, the more they save.

## THE BASE — $299/mo + $500 setup
**Every partner starts here. Non-negotiable.**

What they get:
- UpTend platform access (partner dashboard, basic CRM, lead routing)
- George AI chat intake on their UpTend pages
- Listed on UpTend marketplace (customers can find them)
- Basic job scheduling (calendar view, manual create/assign)
- Job status tracking (scheduled → complete)
- Customer booking confirmations
- 5% platform job fee on completed jobs routed through UpTend
- 2% fee on partner-sourced jobs using the platform

This is the floor. It gets them on the platform with George handling chat and leads flowing in. Everything else is added based on their needs.
