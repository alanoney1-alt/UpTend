## 1. Discovery Questions by Vertical

_Use these questions during your first meeting or discovery call. Start with the universal questions, then dive into the vertical specific ones. We cover every major home service category so you walk in understanding THEIR trade._

### Universal Questions (Ask Every Prospect)

_Organized by phase. Work through these naturally in conversation. You don't need to ask every single one, but cover each phase._

#### Phase 1: Their World

1. What's your company name, and what services do you offer?
2. How long have you been in business?
3. How many techs or crew members do you have?
4. What's your service area? How far do you go?
5. What's your average ticket size?
6. Do you have seasonal patterns? Busy months vs. slow months?
7. How many calls or leads do you get per week?

#### Phase 2: How Customers Find Them

8. Where do most of your leads come from right now? (Referrals, Angi, Thumbtack, Google, etc.)
9. What are you currently spending on Angi, Thumbtack, Google Ads, or other lead sources per month?
10. How many do you lose because nobody picked up or responded in time?
11. Does your website show up when someone Googles your service + your city?
12. When was the last time your website was updated?
13. Do you know what your competitors are charging?
14. Are there specific neighborhoods where you want more work?

#### Phase 3: What Happens When a Lead Comes In

15. How do you handle after hours calls and weekend inquiries?
16. How fast do you respond to online inquiries?
17. Who follows up if they don't book on the first call?
18. How many estimates do you do that don't convert? How many truck rolls just for quotes?
19. How do you assign jobs to your crew right now?
20. What software do you use for scheduling and dispatch?
21. Can your customers check job status or pay online?

#### Phase 4: After the Job

22. How do you get Google reviews from customers?
23. Do you email or text past customers?
24. What percentage of your revenue is repeat/maintenance business?
25. Do your customers ever ask for services you don't offer?
26. Do you lose big jobs because of sticker shock? Do you offer payment options?

#### Phase 5: Marketing & Visibility

27. Who manages your social media, if anyone?
28. How often do you post on social media?
29. Have you tried video content? Before and afters?
30. When was the last time you updated your Google Business Profile?
31. How do you track your marketing ROI?

#### Phase 6: Tools & Spend

32. Walk me through every tool you're paying for monthly.
33. If you added it all up, what's the total?
34. What's working and what's not?

#### Phase 7: Goals

35. What's your single biggest pain point right now?
36. If you could fix one thing about your business tomorrow, what would it be?
37. Where do you want to be in 12 months? Revenue? Team size?
38. What's holding you back from getting there?
