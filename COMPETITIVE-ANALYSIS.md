### Top Priority Gaps to Close

1. **Job Guarantee** ‚Äî Nearly every major marketplace offers some form of satisfaction/money-back guarantee. UpTend needs one to compete on trust.
2. **Voice AI Channel** ‚Äî Adding phone-based Bud (voice AI) would capture demographics that prefer calling and handle after-hours intake.

5. **B2B Channel** ‚Äî Property managers and Airbnb hosts are high-volume repeat customers. A partner API/portal is a growth lever.
6. **Digital Contracts** ‚Äî Pre-work authorization signing adds professionalism and legal protection.

## Strategic Recommendations

### Near-Term (Next 3-6 Months)
1. **Launch a satisfaction guarantee** ‚Äî Even $500 to start. Critical for trust-building in Orlando market launch.
2. **Add voice AI for Bud** ‚Äî Phone intake channel. Captures after-hours and phone-preferred customers.
3. **Implement recurring service plans** ‚Äî Subscription for weekly/biweekly/monthly cleaning, lawn care. Massive retention driver.
4. **Digital pre-work contracts** ‚Äî Scope agreement signed before pro arrives. Reduces disputes.
5. **Project cost guides** ‚Äî SEO content play. "How much does pressure washing cost in Orlando?" drives organic traffic.

### Medium-Term (6-12 Months)
6. **B2B partner API** ‚Äî Property managers, Airbnb hosts, real estate agents can embed UpTend services.
7. **Same-day service guarantee** ‚Äî For select high-supply categories (cleaning, junk removal).
8. **Home profile / CRM** ‚Äî Let homeowners track their home's service history, warranties, upcoming maintenance.
9. **DIY triage in photo-quote** ‚Äî "You could fix this yourself (here's how) ‚Äî or book a pro for $X."
10. **Expand service area** ‚Äî Use the Orlando playbook to launch in Tampa, Miami, Jacksonville.

### Long-Term (12+ Months)
11. **Neighbor/community features** ‚Äî Local service recommendations, bulk discounts for neighborhoods.
12. **Insurance partnerships** ‚Äî Bundle home insurance with UpTend service discounts (Porch model).
13. **Emergency dispatch mode** ‚Äî 24/7 urgent service for water damage, lockouts, etc. with premium pricing.
