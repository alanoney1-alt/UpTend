Go-to-Market

B2B-Led Growth: HOAs and Property Managers Are the Channel

One HOA partnership delivers hundreds of customers at near-zero acquisition cost.

The B2B Playbook

Step 1: HOA Boards

Board members face liability for contractor recommendations. UpTend becomes the official platform. Board endorsement = instant credibility + bulk customer access. Residents self-serve through George.

Step 2: Property Managers

PMs manage 50-200+ properties with scattered vendors and no pricing benchmarks. UpTend gives them a single dashboard with AI-powered vendor management. Tenants request maintenance through George.

Step 3: Home Service Businesses

Existing companies load employees as off-hours pros. Business earns revenue share. Workers earn side income. UpTend gets pre-vetted supply at zero recruiting cost.

Why B2B First

  • Unit economics: One HOA deal = 200-500 households. CAC drops to near zero per customer.
  • Trust transfer: Board endorsement > any ad campaign. Institutional credibility on day one.
  • Recurring revenue: Monthly platform contracts + per-transaction fees. Predictable.
  • Supply solves itself: Guaranteed customer volume attracts quality pros.
  • Data density: Hundreds of homes in one community = pricing intelligence that compounds.
